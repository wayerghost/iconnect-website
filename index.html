About Us

Connecting passion with purpose, we are more than just a student organization.

Our Vision

i-Connect Student Society, as the sole computer-based organization in the University of Rizal System – Binangonan shall promote digital literacy, technical competency and technological capability among students that shall outgrow by the year 2016.

Our Mission

i-Connect Student Society exists to provide supplementary knowledge in line with Information and Communication Technology, always promote computer courses that will unite the youth under the banner of leadership, technical excellence, and ethical conduct among themselves, while fostering community partnerships that enhance shared learning and social responsibility.
